Rhinitis Care System

Digital symptom assessment & follow-up support
About this system

The Rhinitis Care System is a digital health platform designed to help patients assess nasal allergy symptoms and support clinical decision-making.

By collecting symptom severity, frequency, and environmental factors, the system provides structured recommendations based on clinical guidelines and enables doctors to monitor patient progress over time.

How it works
1.
Create an account and complete your personal and symptom history.
2.
Fill in the symptom assessment form (VAS score, frequency, triggers).
3.
Receive evidence-based treatment recommendations immediately.
4.
Doctors can review symptom trends, history, and treatment response.
